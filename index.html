About Me

Nurturing Every Child's Potential

Hello! I'm Deepa Singh, a passionate and dedicated special needs educator with a Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) and over 8 years of hands-on experience working with children across the spectrum of learning and developmental differences.

My journey began with a simple belief: every child deserves to be seen, heard, and celebrated for who they are. I specialize in creating individualized, inclusive learning environments where children with autism, Down syndrome, dyslexia, ADHD, cerebral palsy, and other differently-abled conditions can thrive.

I work closely with families as partners — because the most powerful changes happen when educators and caregivers move together with understanding, patience, and love.

Parent Resource Hub

Guides to Support Your Child

A curated collection of books, organizations, websites, and everyday tips to help you support and celebrate your specially-abled child.

📖

Recommended Books

  • The Reason I Jump — Naoki Higashida A profound first-person account of life with autism, offering parents deep insight into their child's inner world.
  • Uniquely Human — Barry M. Prizant A compassionate and research-backed guide to understanding and supporting autistic children.
  • The Out-of-Sync Child — Carol Kranowitz Essential reading for parents of children with sensory processing differences.
  • Overcoming Dyslexia — Sally Shaywitz A trusted guide for understanding and supporting children with dyslexia.
🏛️

Support Organizations

  • Action for Autism (India) India's premier autism organization offering resources, training, and a directory of support services.
  • National Trust (India) Government body supporting persons with autism, cerebral palsy, mental retardation, and multiple disabilities.
  • ADAPT (Able Disabled All People Together) Mumbai-based organization working across the spectrum of disability, rehabilitation, and inclusion.
  • Down Syndrome Federation of India A network of parent groups and organizations supporting families of children with Down syndrome.
💻

Online Platforms

  • Understood.org A world-class resource for parents of children with learning and thinking differences — articles, tools, and community.
  • Khan Academy (Accessible Learning) Free, self-paced academic content with visual and interactive lessons ideal for differently-abled learners.
  • AutismSpeaks.org A comprehensive resource hub including tools, guides, and research for autism families.
  • CDC — Learn the Signs. Act Early. Free developmental milestone tracking tools and early intervention resources from the CDC.
💡

Everyday Tips for Parents

  • Celebrate the small wins. Every milestone — however small — is a giant leap. Build a culture of celebration at home.
  • Use visual schedules. Predictability reduces anxiety. Picture-based daily routines give children a sense of safety and control.
  • Follow the child's lead. Engage in activities your child loves. Interest-led learning is the most powerful kind.
  • Take care of yourself too. You cannot pour from an empty cup. Seek support, rest, and community for your own wellbeing.
